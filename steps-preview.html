YOUR TEAM

Your staff are already there.
Here's how to set them up.

1
Staff arrive automatically
Veras pulls your team from Paycor, ADP, or your HRIS. No manual entry — profiles are already there when you log in.
2
Set up their schedule
Find the staff member → click Add under the Schedule column. Choose Fixed Days, Rotation, or Flexible (PRN) and Veras handles the rest.
3
Open their profile for details
Click their name to edit contact info, add credentials, certifications, and skills — or manually add someone the HRIS missed.
4
Send them the app
One tap sends the download link. They're on Veras — shifts visible, requests come to you, no more personal texts.
